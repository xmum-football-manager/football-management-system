# Exceed Football Cup — Design Notes

## Colour Palette Rules
- **DO NOT** use orange/gold (`#E8A012` or similar amber) as the auxiliary accent colour — it clashes with the deep navy backgrounds and reads as "warning" rather than "premium sport".
- **Main colour**: Deep navy blues — use the darkest shade (`#060C1C`) as the base background, stepping up through the navy scale for surfaces.
- **Accent colour**: Electric blue → cyan (`#1E78F0` → `#38B4FF`). Use the electric blue as the primary CTA/interactive accent and the bright cyan as the hover/glow state. This matches the vibrant blue sports-graphic aesthetic in the visual concept reference.
- **Auxiliary**: Cyan highlights, white text on dark, red for live/danger states only.

## Reference Aesthetic
Sports graphics visual concept: very dark navy base, electric blue accent, cyan pop highlights, high contrast white type.
No warm colours in the primary palette — keep it cool and authoritative.
